Welcome to Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a non-profit organization located in Ocala, Florida. We are dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of sick, injured, or orphaned wildlife. We work with local veterinarians, wildlifeagencies, and the public to help save countless animals every year.

Our mission is to give every animal a second chance at life in the wild.

If you find an animal in need, please contact us immediately.
